∵bx′+ry′=(b,r) ,(a,b)=(b,r)\because bx'+ry'=(b,r)\ , (a,b)=(b,r)∵bx′+ry′=(b,r) ,(a,b)=(b,r)
∴bx′+ry′=(a,b)\therefore bx'+ry'=(a,b)∴bx′+ry′=(a,b)
∵a=bq+r ,x=y′,y=x′−qy′\because a=bq+r\ ,x=y',y=x'-qy'∵a=bq+r ,x=y′,y=x′−qy′
∴ax+by=(bq+r)y′+b(x′−qy′)=bx′+ry′\therefore ax+by=(bq+r)y'+b(x'-qy')=bx'+ry'∴ax+by=(bq+r)y′+b(x′−qy′)=bx′+ry′
∴ax+by=(a,b)\therefore ax+by=(a,b)∴ax+by=(a,b)
